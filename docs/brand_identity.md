# Luxe Estate — Brand Identity & Planning

## 1. Website Purpose
To serve as the premier digital platform for luxury real estate, offering a cinematic, high-end experience that connects elite buyers with exclusive properties worldwide.

## 2. Target Audience
- High-net-worth individuals (HNWIs) and ultra-high-net-worth individuals (UHNWIs).
- Celebrities, executives, and investors looking for premium real estate.
- Architecture enthusiasts and luxury lifestyle connoisseurs.

## 3. Brand Identity
- **Personality**: Luxury, Minimal, Editorial, Modern, Timeless, Architectural, Exclusive, Elegant, Premium.
- **Visuals**: Large white space, editorial layouts, strong typography, balanced composition, luxury color palette.

## 4. User Personas
- **The Investor**: Looking for high-yield luxury properties, values quick access to data and architectural blueprints.
- **The Lifestyle Buyer**: Looking for a second home, values location, amenities, and visual storytelling.

## 5. Design System Tokens

### Colors
- Primary: `#090909`
- Secondary: `#111111`
- Cards: `#171717`
- Text: `#FFFFFF`
- Muted: `#CFCFCF`
- Accent: `#C8A86B`
- Hover Accent: `#E7C58A`
- Glass: `rgba(255,255,255,.08)`

### Typography
- Heading Fonts: Canela, PP Editorial, Neue Montreal, General Sans
- Body Font: Inter

### System
- Spacing: Elegant, open, utilizing large padding and margins (e.g., 4rem, 8rem, 12rem gaps).
- Radius: Minimal or zero border-radius for an architectural, brutalist-luxury feel.
- Motion: Slow, smooth, luxury easing (Cubic Bezier: 0.76, 0, 0.24, 1).
